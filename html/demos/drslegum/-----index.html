// About Us

Why DRS Legum "Law offices"?

DRS Legum "Law offices" is a leading full-service law firm based in Delhi-NCR, India, formed by Mr. Deepak Kumar Bansal and Mr. Sandeep Tomar for serving clients in a broad spectrum of legal issues and challenges. We focus on solutions through a unique holistic approach that blends its expertise and decades of experience in core practice areas.

We are renowned for our commitment to excellence and for our ability to find innovative solutions to the most complex of legal problems. Our clients recognize and value this. To-date, DRS Legum continues to distinguish itself as a result-oriented law firm.

  • The one-point destination for all legal needs
  • Pan India availability and visibility
  • Team of dedicated lawyers and other professionals
  • Cost-effective and result oriented approach
  • The high success rate in Litigation
  • The efficient client follows up, and Status updates.

DRS Legum "Law offices" vision is people-centric, and this is primarily reflected in the firm's focus on developing and nurturing long-term relationships with clients by providing optimal solutions in a practical, qualitative, and cost- efficient manner. The firm's in-depth expertise, immediate availability, geographic reach, transparent approach, and the involvement of senior partners in all assignments. We have multiple offices in Delhi and channel partners all over India. We can help you with the Litigation and consultation all over India.
